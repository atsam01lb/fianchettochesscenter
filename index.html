Who We Are

A Home for the Royal Game.

Fianchetto Chess Center was founded with a single mission: to grow a community where chess is more than a pastime — it is a discipline, an art form, and a lifelong companion. From our first open in 2015 to the championships we host today, every move forward has been built on that idea.

We welcome players of every age and rating. Whether you are learning how the knight moves for the first time or preparing for a national tournament, you will find a board, a coach, and an opponent worthy of your time at Fianchetto.

Why Fianchetto

Built on Three Principles.

Every class, tournament, and lesson at Fianchetto is shaped by what we believe makes a great chess community.

01

Certified Coaches

Our coaching team holds national titles and years of competitive experience. You learn from players who have lived what they teach.

02

Official Tournaments

Rated events organized to professional standards. Every game counts toward your official rating and your progress.

03

All Ages Welcome
